Project Proposals & Ideas

Flagship Project Idea

Skill Exchange Platform for Students

The Problem

Many students possess valuable skills (coding, design, video editing, public speaking), but there is no centralized platform to discover peers with complementary skills.

The Solution

A campus-wide skill exchange platform where students can teach, learn, collaborate, and exchange skills with one another.

Why This Matters

Students often possess valuable skills but lack a structured platform to exchange knowledge and collaborate. This platform creates a peer-to-peer learning ecosystem where students can teach, learn, and grow together while building valuable connections.

Core Features
  • Skill Discovery
  • AI-Based Skill Matching
  • Peer Learning Sessions
  • Student Profiles
  • Ratings & Feedback
  • Community Building
Impact

Promotes collaborative learning, networking, and peer-to-peer education within campuses.
